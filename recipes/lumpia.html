Lumpia Recipe

Ingredients

  • 500g ground pork or beef
  • 1 cup finely chopped carrots
  • 1 cup finely chopped cabbage
  • 1 small onion, finely chopped
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 2 tablespoons soy sauce
  • 1 tablespoon oyster sauce
  • 1 egg, beaten
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • 25 lumpia wrappers
  • Cooking oil for frying

Steps

  1. In a large bowl, combine the ground pork, chopped carrots, cabbage, onion, garlic, soy sauce, oyster sauce, beaten egg, salt, and pepper. Mix well until all ingredients are thoroughly combined.
  2. Lay a lumpia wrapper on a flat surface. Place about 2 tablespoons of the filling near one corner of the wrapper, shaping it into a log.
  3. Fold the corner of the wrapper over the filling, then fold in the sides and roll tightly to form a spring roll. Moisten the edge with water to seal. Repeat with the remaining filling and wrappers.
  4. Heat the cooking oil in a deep pan over medium heat. Fry the lumpia in batches until they are golden brown and crispy, about 3-5 minutes per batch. Drain on paper towels.
  5. Serve the lumpia hot with sweet and sour dipping sauce. Enjoy!
