Pansit

Description

Pancit is a traditional Filipino noodle dish known for its delicious blend of flavors and textures. This dish features stir-fried noodles with a mix of vegetables, meat, and sometimes seafood. Pancit is often served at celebrations and gatherings, symbolizing long life and prosperity.

Ingredients

  • 200g Rice Noodles
  • 100g Chicken Breast, sliced
  • 100g shrimp, peeled and deveined
  • 1 cup shredded cabbage
  • 1 carrot, julienned
  • 1 bell pepper, sliced
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 small onion, chopped
  • 2 tablespoons soy sauce
  • 1 tablespoon oyster sauce
  • 1 cup chicken broth
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Lemon wedges for serving

Steps

  1. Cook the spaghetti in a large pot of boiling salted water until al dente. Reserve a cup of pasta water, then drain the spaghetti.
  2. In a large skillet, cook the pancetta over medium heat until crispy. Add the minced garlic and cook for another minute until fragrant.
  3. In a bowl, whisk together the eggs and grated Parmesan cheese.
  4. Add the cooked spaghetti to the skillet with the pancetta and garlic, tossing to combine.
  5. Remove the skillet from heat and quickly pour the egg and cheese mixture over the pasta, tossing vigorously to create a creamy sauce. Add reserved pasta water as needed to reach desired consistency.
  6. Season with salt and black pepper to taste. Garnish with chopped fresh parsley if desired.
  7. Serve immediately and enjoy your delicious Spaghetti Carbonara!
